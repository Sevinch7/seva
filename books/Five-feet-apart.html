Five Feet Apart

A Heartbreaking Love Story - Book Summary

About the Book

Five Feet Apart, written by Rachael Lippincott with Mikki Daughtry and Tobias Iaconis, is a poignant contemporary romance novel. It follows the story of Stella Grant and Will Newman, two teenagers with cystic fibrosis who fall in love but must stay six feet apart to avoid life-threatening cross-infection. The story beautifully explores themes of love, resilience, and the painful sacrifices required in the face of illness.

Key Themes

  • Love and Sacrifice: The novel captures the heart-wrenching balance between love and the need to protect one another.
  • Living with Illness: Provides an empathetic view of life with cystic fibrosis and the challenges it brings.
  • Resilience: Highlights the strength required to live fully despite limitations and uncertainties.

Why You Should Read It

Five Feet Apart is a deeply emotional read that touches on universal themes of love, loss, and human connection. Whether you're drawn to romance, stories of perseverance, or are curious about living with a chronic condition, this book is both heartwarming and heartbreaking in equal measure.
